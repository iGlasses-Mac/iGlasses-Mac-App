## Overview  

iGlasses is a professional-grade webcam enhancement application designed for Apple platforms that provides full control over video quality in real-time. It allows users to improve the appearance of their video for calls, streaming, and recordings across applications like Zoom, Skype, FaceTime, and OBS.  

The software offers precise adjustments for brightness, contrast, exposure, saturation, and color balance, giving you the ability to fine-tune your video to look exactly how you want. Advanced features like digital zoom, cropping, mirror mode, and special visual effects make it easy to create a polished, professional video feed for any scenario.  

iGlasses is ideal for content creators, educators, remote workers, and anyone who relies on high-quality video communication. It supports multiple webcams and allows users to switch seamlessly between cameras while keeping unique settings for each device. Real-time preview ensures that every adjustment is visible immediately, enabling quick fine-tuning without interrupting your workflow.  

The application also includes features like orientation flip, mirror mode, and enhanced color correction for consistent image quality across different lighting conditions. Whether you're streaming live events, recording tutorials, or hosting virtual meetings, iGlasses guarantees clear, vibrant, and visually engaging video output. Its intuitive interface makes complex adjustments easy for both beginners and professionals, ensuring that your webcam performance always looks its best.
